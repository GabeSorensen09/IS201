Shop Loval, support local About Our community

I recently moved to Provo, Utah, and immediately fell in love with the local community. I have wanted to be a small business owner since I was young. While that is still a work in progress, I find it very fulfilling to support our local economy. When shopping for a friend or exploring something new, see if you can find it in your backyard. You will be pleasantly surprised at the diverse selection we have here. While it isn't what you would typically find at a big box store or an online marketplace, shopping small is a way you can show that you care about your local community.
